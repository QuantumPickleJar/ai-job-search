# Disallowed Claims

Do not make any claim unless it is explicitly supported by verified profile facts.

## Explicitly Disallowed Unless Verified

- Do not claim senior developer experience.
- Do not claim formal team lead authority unless verified.
- Do not claim Kubernetes production ownership unless verified.
- Do not claim AWS production ownership unless verified.
- Do not claim Azure production ownership unless verified.
- Do not claim security clearance.
- Do not claim professional machine learning engineer experience.
- Do not claim management experience.
- Do not claim years of experience beyond what is supported.
- Do not claim any certification not explicitly listed.

## Technology Claim Boundaries

- Docker may be described only at the level supported by actual use.
- CI/CD should be described as exposure unless verified as hands-on ownership.
- Raspberry Pi / Arduino should be described as familiarity unless project evidence supports more.
- Cloud, Kubernetes, security, and machine learning claims require explicit evidence before use.

## Application Writing Rules

- If a job asks for a missing skill, acknowledge adjacent experience honestly.
- Prefer "exposure to" or "familiarity with" when experience is limited.
- Do not convert coursework, personal projects, or internship exposure into senior professional ownership.
- Flag unsupported claims as questions instead of writing them into resumes or cover letters.
